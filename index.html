About the Book

Eat Right 4 Your Type by Dr. Peter J. D’Adamo is a groundbreaking book that reveals how your blood type (O, A, B, or AB) is the key to understanding which foods are highly beneficial, neutral, or harmful to your body.

Based on evolutionary biology, lectin science, and digestive chemistry, the book shows that the same food can be medicine for one blood type and a toxin for another. It also covers personalized exercise recommendations, stress responses, weight-loss strategies, and long-term health insights tailored to each blood type.

Over 20 years later, millions of people worldwide still use these principles to improve energy, digestion, immunity, and weight management.
